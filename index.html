About the Artist

Landscape painting has not always been my favourite subject and something which has taken a lot of practice. First starting to paint landscape merely as a backdrop in wildlife paintings, I have discovered a love of painting sunsets, clouds and water.

Being from Northern Ireland where the landscape around the North Antrim coast is stunning, I've had much inspiration and motivation to get better!

I will hopefully continue to push my boundaries as an artist by exploring new themes and trying to get better at the subjects I find more difficult.
